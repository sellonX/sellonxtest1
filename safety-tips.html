Safety Tips

Stay safe while buying and selling on SellonX

Meet in Safe Public Places

Always meet buyers or sellers in busy, well-lit public places like shopping malls, coffee shops, or police station parking lots. Avoid meeting at your home or in secluded areas, especially for high-value items.

Do

  • Choose busy public locations
  • Meet during daylight hours
  • Tell a friend or family member where you're going
  • Consider bringing someone with you

Don't

  • Meet at your home for first interactions
  • Meet in secluded areas
  • Meet late at night
  • Share your home address unnecessarily

Be Careful with Payments

Cash is often the safest payment method for in-person transactions. Be extremely cautious with payment requests that seem unusual or suspicious. Never send money to someone you haven't met, especially via wire transfer or gift cards.

Do

  • Use cash for in-person transactions
  • Count money in front of the seller/buyer
  • Check for counterfeit bills if accepting cash
  • Consider using secure payment apps for larger transactions

Don't

  • Send money to people you haven't met
  • Use wire transfers to unknown recipients
  • Accept checks from strangers
  • Fall for overpayment scams

Inspect Before You Buy

Always thoroughly inspect items before purchasing. Test electronics to ensure they work properly. For vehicles, consider having a mechanic inspect them. Don't rush the inspection process, and don't be afraid to ask questions.

Do

  • Test electronics before paying
  • Inspect items carefully for damage
  • Ask for original receipts when possible
  • Verify that the item matches the description

Don't

  • Rush through inspections
  • Feel pressured to buy immediately
  • Pay before inspecting the item
  • Ignore signs of damage or tampering

Protect Your Personal Information

Be cautious about sharing personal information. There's rarely a legitimate reason for someone to need your banking details, ID number, or home address before meeting. Share only what's necessary to complete the transaction.

Do

  • Use the SellonX messaging system initially
  • Share only necessary contact information
  • Create a separate email for classifieds if needed
  • Trust your instincts if something feels wrong

Don't

  • Share banking or financial information
  • Provide copies of ID documents
  • Give out your home address unnecessarily
  • Share personal details not related to the transaction

Recognize Common Scams

Be aware of common scams like fake payment emails, overpayment scams, and requests to wire money. If a deal seems too good to be true, it probably is. Trust your instincts and walk away from any transaction that feels suspicious.

Watch Out For:

  • Overpayment scams: Someone sends you a check for more than the asking price and asks you to wire back the difference.
  • Fake payment emails: Emails claiming payment has been sent when it hasn't.
  • Shipping scams: Requests to ship items before payment is confirmed.
  • Fake escrow services: Links to fraudulent escrow websites.
  • Sob stories: Emotional appeals designed to make you lower your guard.

Stay Connected During Meetings

Keep your phone charged and accessible when meeting buyers or sellers. Consider sharing your location with a trusted friend or family member. If possible, bring someone with you, especially for high-value transactions.

Meeting Checklist:

  • Tell someone where you're going and who you're meeting
  • Share your location with a trusted contact
  • Keep your phone fully charged
  • Bring a friend for high-value transactions
  • Meet in a public place with security cameras if possible
  • Trust your instincts - leave if something feels wrong

Your Safety is Our Priority

At SellonX, we're committed to creating a safe marketplace for our community. While most transactions go smoothly, staying vigilant helps ensure a positive experience. If you encounter suspicious activity on our platform, please report it immediately.
